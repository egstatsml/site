Errata Corrected on arXiv version of paper.

\textbf{Eqn 3.30} on page 63 for leap frog integration, the step should be $\epsilon/2$. I am not happy with the notation there. Instead use,

\begin{align*}
  v_i(t + \frac{\epsilon}{2}) = v_i(t) + \frac{\epsilon}{2} \dfrac{dv_{i}(t)}{dt},
  \\
  w_i(t + \epsilon) = w_i(t) + \epsilon \frac{dw_{i}(t + \epsilon /2)}{dt},
  \\
  v_i(t + \epsilon) = v_i(t +\frac{\epsilon}{2}) + \frac{\epsilon}{2} \dfrac{dv_{i}(t + \epsilon)}{dt}.
\end{align*}

\textbf{Eqn. 3.48} on page 68, is missing the logarithms, it should read,
\begin{equation*}
    \sigma_{t+1} =
                 \sigma_t + \sigma_t^2 \Big[ \big( \dfrac{\partial \log p(\mathcal{D}_{t+1})}{\partial \mu_t} \big)^2 -
                 2 \dfrac{\partial \log p(\mathcal{D}_{t+1})}{ \partial \sigma} \Big].
\end{equation*}


\textbf{Eqn. 3.53} on page 69, the $e$ should be a $\mathbb{E}$
  
For the $\alpha$ divergence on page 75, the limits to relate to forward/reverse KL divergence and Hellinger distance were not correct. It should be, $KL(q|p): \alpha \rightarrow 0, KL(p|q): \alpha \rightarrow 1,$ Hellinger: $ \alpha \rightarrow 0.5$.

\textbf{Eq. 3.58} missing factor of $1/2$

\textbf{Eq. 3.63} page 79 missing $\exp$ in numerator for softmax.
